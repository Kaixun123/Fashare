FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


MOST POPULAR QUESTIONS


Q: What is this website for?

Fashare is a website made for people to share their individual styles they would usually wear daily or for an important occasion.


Q: Must I need an account to use this site?

You can view post from the trending page but would need to view the home page and to rent from the owners.


Q: How do I rent?

You are required to set up an account before you rent and you can find more information on the "How to Rent" section.


Q: Who can I contact for help or assistance?

You can contact us through our email address and helplines number that all can be found on the "Contact Us" section.


Q: What can I do on this website?

You can browse post made by your friends and rent from them or you can create your own post and have people rent from you.

  • HOW TO RENT

    Step 1: Search for the item(s) you are looking for

    You can find them in post made by friends/followers or throught the trending and home pages.


    Step 2: Click on the item you want to rent

    It is located on the post itself. Do remember to check the size and the description of each post check if the outfit fits you.


    Step 3: Message the owner/user

    We're not gonna do it for you, so get messaging!!


    Step 4: Choose preferred date and send request to rent

    Only once both the owner and renter both agree on the things needed.


    Step 5: Procced with payment

    Payment can done through Visa/MasterCard or even through bank transfer(ibanking)


    Step 6: Meet up with the owner and you're done!!

    Remeber to be friendly to each other

  • ABOUT US


    We are five poly students from Nanyang Polytechnic (NYP) who have created this website to help students who may be in need of a dress/outfit that she/he may need for an important event or presentation. We got the inspiration when we start to notice that student in our class did not really brother to dress up nicely and we wanted to change that.


    Fashare is a fashion website that allows students to share their looks or their styles with other students and even allow for renting with other students as well which can students earn some money well they are studying.


    Our goal is for people to be able to share what the type of outfit they like to share to others and get more people to start dress up nicely, promoting a more fashionable environment.
